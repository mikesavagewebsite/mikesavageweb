The Process

How to Apply

1

Read the Audition Brief

Download the audition pack when available. It includes project details, character descriptions, and all required materials.

2

Prepare Your Materials

Follow the instructions in the pack. This typically includes a self-tape or in-person audition, a headshot, and a short bio.

3

Submit Your Application

Send your materials to the contact provided in the audition brief before the stated deadline.

4

Wait for a Response

Our casting team reviews all submissions. Shortlisted candidates will be contacted directly for callbacks.
