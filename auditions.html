How to Apply

  • 1

    Read the Audition Brief

    Download the audition pack when available. It contains the project title, character descriptions, eligibility criteria, audition dates, and all required materials.

  • 2

    Prepare Your Materials

    Prepare a self-tape or attend in person as directed. You will typically need a headshot, a short bio, and the required audition piece as described in the brief.

  • 3

    Submit Your Application

    Send your materials to the contact address provided in the audition brief. Make sure all submissions are complete and sent before the stated deadline.

  • 4

    Wait for a Response

    Our casting team reviews every submission carefully. Shortlisted candidates will be contacted directly with details for the next stage.
