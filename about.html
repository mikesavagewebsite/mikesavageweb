About Mike Savage Studios

Who we are, what we believe, and why we create

Who We Are

Mikesavage Studios is a powerhouse in film, comedy, and original entertainment, dedicated to creating bold, engaging content that resonates with audiences.

From laugh-out-loud comedies to boundary-pushing films, the studio brings fresh, fearless storytelling to life with a distinct creative edge. Every project is crafted to entertain, provoke thought, and leave a lasting impression.

Whether producing short films, viral skits, or full-length features, Mikesavage Studios stays true to its identity — real, raw, and relentlessly entertaining.

Welcome to a world where creativity meets savage energy.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To create bold, original content — from films to comedy — that entertains, provokes thought, and connects deeply with audiences through fearless storytelling and savage creative energy.

Our Vision

To build Mikesavage Studios into a leading entertainment powerhouse that shapes culture, amplifies authentic voices, and delivers world-class content to global audiences.

Meet the Founder

ONAOLAPO OLANIYI MICHEAL
Founder & Filmmaker

Onaolapo Olaniyi Micheal, popularly known as Mikesavage, is a filmmaker, actor, and content creator driven by a passion for storytelling and performance.

With a strong presence in both film and digital entertainment, he has built a reputation for delivering content that connects with audiences through authenticity, humor, and originality. His work reflects a commitment to pushing creative boundaries while staying true to his unique voice.

Through Mikesavage Studios, he continues to shape stories that entertain, inspire, and stand out in today's evolving media landscape.
