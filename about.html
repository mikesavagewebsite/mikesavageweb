Who We Are

About Mike Savage Studios

Mike Savage Studios is an independent film and television production company dedicated to creating stories that matter. Our work is rooted in authentic human experience — the triumphs, struggles, relationships, and quiet moments that shape who we become.

We believe that the most powerful stories are those that feel true. Whether it's a coming-of-age drama, a gripping thriller, a romantic short film, or an ambitious series, our commitment is the same: tell it honestly, tell it well, and make it resonate long after the screen goes dark.

Founded by Mike Savage, the studio brings together passionate filmmakers, writers, and performers who share a singular vision — to produce work of lasting artistic and cultural value. Every project we undertake is approached with intentionality, craft, and a deep respect for the audience.

We are building a body of work that reflects the full spectrum of human experience. We invite you to be a part of this journey — as a viewer, a collaborator, or a storyteller in your own right.

Our Mission

Why We Tell Stories

Every story we tell begins with a question: does this matter? We measure our work not by budgets or production scale, but by emotional truth. If a story makes someone feel seen, heard, or understood — it has done its job.

We are committed to amplifying voices that are often overlooked, telling stories from communities that rarely see themselves on screen. Mike Savage Studios exists to change that, one production at a time.

Join us on this journey. Watch our films. Share our stories. And if you have a story you believe the world needs to hear, reach out — we'd love to hear from you.
