LOVE & BETRAYAL

📅 March 19, 2026 🕐 3 min read 🎬 Mike Savage Studios

We are proud to present our latest production — Love & Betrayal — a gripping drama that explores the raw, complex emotions of love, loyalty, and deception.

What happens when the people we trust the most become the source of our deepest pain? Love & Betrayal takes viewers on an emotional rollercoaster through broken relationships, hidden secrets, and the ultimate cost of trusting the wrong people.

Starring Olaniyi Micheal, Orubor Anthony, Rubby Nku, Stephanie Nwachukwu, and Doris Dan, this film delivers powerhouse performances that will keep you glued to your screen from start to finish.

Produced by Mike Savage Studios, Love & Betrayal is a testament to our commitment to bold, authentic storytelling that resonates with real human experiences. Watch it now on YouTube — absolutely free.
